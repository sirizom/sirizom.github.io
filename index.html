SUSAMATI is a social business that is bringing innovative and sustainable solutions to the challenges of sanitation in Mozambique. The idea comes as a result of the Biological Urban Sanitation Project (BUSP), which was financed by Via Water, but as our team is very young and eager to innovate and solve environmental problems, after the project, we decided to continue to work as a social enterprise. The purpose of this business is to allow low income families across Africa to have access to modern and sustainable sanitation solutions for an affordable price and also educate their families to adopt good habits towards their sanitary and environmental behavior.

This is an action that we normally do as a regular activity but we also do in partnership with NGOs that have projects on sanitation or environmental education and they hire us to use our experience in field work to go and communicate with the population, the campaigns often take place in markets and open spaces but we also do one on one approach and radio communications. Read more

The first product we put on the market was Pia Fantastica, a toilet that besides being colorful and being the cheapest sanitation solution in the market, uses only one glass of water for each flush. The toilet is produced on a concrete base, presenting the oval-mouthed, being designed to ensure the comfort of the user. It also presents the particularity of having a pipe with 45º slope without siphon which reduces the amount of water required for each discharge, tests performed so far show that PIA FANTASTICA can use no more than 2Dl of water in each flush.
